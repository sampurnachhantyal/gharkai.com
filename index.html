About Us

Who We Are

Gharkai.com is Kathmandu's premier corporate food management service, dedicated to providing high-quality, home-style meals to businesses and their employees.

We understand the importance of nutritious and delicious meals in maintaining employee satisfaction and productivity.

Our Mission

To revolutionize corporate dining by bringing the comfort and quality of home-cooked meals to the workplace, while providing efficient management solutions for businesses.

We aim to create a seamless food service experience that benefits both companies and their employees.
